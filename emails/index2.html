A Wavetronix Family Event

The Potluck Event of this Galaxy

Revenge of the Fifth

Join us on Friday May 5th for the Wavetronix Potluck event open to all employees and their families.

From 5-7 we will indulge in each others fantastic feasts. While this is a Star Wars themed event, the food you bring does NOT have to be related to Star Wars. If you want to prove to Andy Porter that your smoked chicken is better than his, now is your chance! Or perhaps you just want to show Carlos that your chili is too spicy for him to handle. Or maybe, just maybe, you want to make some Jar-Jar Links because nothing is more than than seeing a group of kids ravenously destroy the prequel icon. Either way, a sign up sheet will be in the breakroom soon.

Also from 5-7 we will be having many kids (and young at heart) activities. There will be a force training academy, a green screen to get your picture taken in the Star Wars universe, lots of color pages, and each kid wil get their own mini-lightsaber (*while supplies last). Bring your whole family!

There will be a costume contest for all ages with prizes awarded before the movie starts.

Then from 7-9 we will watch A New Hope in the Globe rooms. Bring your own pillows, blankets and snacks to lounge out picnic style. Popcorn will be provided!

If you have any questions, please ask myself, Katy Trent or Lara Anderson.

We hope to see you all there!
